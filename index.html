UCLA Swipe Swap

Gain access to UCLA’s diverse dining options in a few clicks. Swipe swap is an interface for sharing meal swipes between on & off campus students.

Epicuria

A Mediterranean marketplace guided by the philosophy of Epicureanism: the idea that the pursuit of pleasure and comfort in relation to our attitude to food and how we prepare it can be found by living a life of simplicity.

Feast

Treat your senses to the flavors and aromas of Asian cuisine by joining us for dinner at The Spice Kitchen at FEAST. The Spice Kitchen is a pop-up style concept that features a rotating menu served at a single platform. Guests may exchange one meal plan swipe for a single meal. FEAST at Rieber is located in Rieber Hall.

De Neve

De Neve celebrates the amazing diversity of foods inspired by North, Central, and South America. You’ll be digging into flavorful menu selections such as Chicago-style deep dish pizza, Texas barbecue brisket, poutine, and Cincinnati chili spaghetti. With its ample seating and central location, De Neve is a great place to meet up with friends for a great meal and great conversation. De Neve is located in De Neve Plaza.

Bruin Plate

Bruin Plate is a creative, honest and responsible eatery that provides seasonal, innovative and sustainable cuisine with mouth-watering results. Even the desserts are healthy! Bruin Plate’s large floor-to-ceiling windows offer gorgeous views of campus and lots of natural light.

Rendezvous

Rendezvous East features foods from across Asia fused with American inspiration, such as ramen, sushi bowls and curry, boba and milk tea, while Rendezvous West focuses on Latin foods like custom-made tacos, burritos, enchiladas and nachos, with a creative flair and influence from regions throughout North, Central and South America.

Study

This European-style artisanal bakery café merges amazing Northern European influenced food with an equally amazing study space. The Study offers private study carrels, quiet reading rooms, and discussion rooms wired with state-of-the-art technology and writable wall surfaces.
